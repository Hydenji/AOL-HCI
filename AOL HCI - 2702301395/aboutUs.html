Welcome to Hythering's Catering

Hythering is one of the pioneer catering services established in Semarang. We offer a variety of menus for our customers, all verified by the government to ensure that our catering uses hygienic and halal ingredients. Customers can place orders through our website or visit our restaurant directly. When it comes to service, there is no doubt; we strive to serve all customers with politeness and excellence.

Our Story

Hythering was established by Patrick, an alumnus of Binus B75. Upon graduation, Patrick found himself at a crossroads and turned to his close friend Moka for guidance. Recognizing the challenges they faced as students in Semarang, particularly in finding affordable meals, Moka proposed the idea of starting a catering business. This suggestion proved fruitful, and Hythering has since grown into a thriving enterprise with branches in multiple locations.
